Our Vision

"To be a center of excellence in Artificial Intelligence education, fostering innovation, ethical values, and technical proficiency to empower students for developing intelligent solutions that address real-world challenges and contribute positively to society."

Our Mission

  • Develop Student Skills: To provide comprehensive and rigorous practical training in modern Artificial Intelligence and Data Science tools and methodologies.
  • Promote Innovation: To foster an environment that encourages innovative thinking, rigorous research, and hands-on capstone projects.
  • Enhance Industry Readiness: To continuously align our curriculum with industry demands, establishing strong professional networking and robust collaboration.
  • Inculcate Ethics: To embed strong professional ethics, societal responsibility, and a lifelong learning mindset within every aspiring technocrat.
