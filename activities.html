Department Activities

Continuous engagement through co-curricular and extracurricular activities shapes the comprehensive growth of our students. Explore our recent initiatives:

Quiz Competition

A high-energy inter-college Tech Quiz challenging students on recent advancements in AI, general tech knowledge, and coding trivia to foster competitive spirit.

Tech Fest

Our grand annual technical festival 'TechKriti' showcases groundbreaking student projects, robot races, hackathons, and dynamic tech exhibitions drawing crowds from across the region.

Rangoli Competition

A colorful cultural event blending artistic talent with technological themes, where students display mesmerizing rangoli designs inspired by artificial intelligence and digital integration.

Debate Competition

Encouraging critical thinking, eloquent articulation, and constructive logical argumentation surrounding the ethical impacts and futuristic implications of Artificial Intelligence globally.

Road Safety Program

A socially responsible initiative organizing awareness drives on essential traffic rules and helmet importance, instilling civic duties within our young engineers.

Parent Teacher Meeting

A collaborative dialogue connecting faculty and parents to transparently evaluate, discuss, and enhance the academic progression and overall holistic well-being of the students.

Campus Cleanliness Drive

An active student-led initiative dedicated to maintaining a pristine, green, and sustainable campus environment. Through regular clean-up campaigns, our engineers demonstrate true social responsibility and environmental awareness.

Digital Literacy Awareness

An empowering initiative focused on teaching essential digital skills to the community, highlighting safe internet practices, safe digital transactions, and responsible technology usage.
